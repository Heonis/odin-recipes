Chocolate Ice cream Mug Cake

What's better than ice cream on your mug cake? Ice cream in a mug cake! Not just that, you can top it with ice cream, too!. Sounds like heaven!

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup vanilla ice cream, melted
  • 1 ½ tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ¼ teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 scoop vanilla ice cream

Steps

  1. Mix melted ice cream, flour, and baking powder together in a microwave-safe mug. Stir in cocoa powder. Microwave until top of cake looks done, about 90 seconds. Top with vanilla ice cream.
